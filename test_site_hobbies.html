What She Is Usually Found Doing

Ana Lisa lives for dancing and if she is not found coding or developing, she is dancing (in fact sometimes she is found doing both at the same time).

She dances many different styles ranging from Tap to Whacking, but her heart truly lives with Lindy Hop and Vernacular Jazz. She spends the majority of her time listening, dancing, traveling, and performing all for them.

Upcoming Gigs!

Ana Lisa will be dancing at the following events in coming months.

  • Lindyfest-Houston TX:The Best Lindy Hop Experience, Period. Lone Star Championships at Lindyfest is the largest and most dynamic Lindy event of its kind. This year marks the 22nd annual Lindyfest and the 11th annual Lone Star Championships!

  • Lindy Bout-Vancouver BC: Lindy Bout is an awesome weekend of Lindy Hop, competitions, workshops, live music, inspiration, and Western Canadian camaraderie held annually in Vancouver, Canada.

With another year of fantastic music, dancing, practices, and afternoon socials, we're excited to give you everything you love about Balboa, perhaps with a surprise or two along the way. This year's Northwest Balboa Festival will bring the best of what the Pacific Northwest's dance community has to offer.

This only covers the upcoming Spring 2018 season, more events and performances will follow for Summer and Fall.
